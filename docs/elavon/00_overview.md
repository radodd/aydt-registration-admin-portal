# Elavon Payment Gateway — Overview

**Source:** https://developer.elavon.com/products/elavon-payment-gateway/v1/overview

**When to reference:** Any time you need a high-level understanding of EPG capabilities, supported payment types, or integration options. Good starting point before diving into a specific feature.

---

## What It Is

Elavon Payment Gateway (EPG) is a payment processing solution designed for eCommerce and card-not-present transactions. The platform enables businesses to accept payments from credit and debit cards, digital wallets, and bank transfers (in certain regions).

## Key Payment Types

- **One-time payments** — collected through integrated checkout pages or phone orders
- **Payment links** — emailed to customers for online transactions
- **Subscriptions** — recurring charges with customizable billing terms

## Integration Approaches

1. **Hosted Payment Pages (HPP)** — Low-code solutions:
   - **HPP Redirect** — Routes customers to a separate Elavon-hosted payment page
   - **HPP Lightbox** — Overlay payment form on the merchant's existing checkout page
2. **Direct API** — Server-to-server connections for full control; supports all transaction types; increases PCI scope

## Supported Features

- Digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay)
- Card-on-file / stored cards
- Scheduled / recurring payments
- Fraud services
- Surcharge capabilities
- Dynamic currency conversion

## Compliance

- Hosted solutions shield merchants from PCI Level 1 compliance scope
- Direct API integrations place PCI compliance responsibility on the merchant

## Regional Support

United States, Canada, Puerto Rico, United Kingdom, Ireland, Poland.

## Support Channels

- Email support for Europe and North America
- Regional phone lines in Ireland, Poland, UK, and USA (US is 24/7)
